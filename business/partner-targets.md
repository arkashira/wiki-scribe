# Partner Targets
## Introduction
To enhance the wiki-scribe platform and provide a comprehensive solution for solo developers and fan-wiki operators, we will integrate with key SaaS/API partners. This document outlines our partner integration roadmap, highlighting 7 specific targets, their free-tier limits, integration effort, and value-add.

## Partner Integration Roadmap

1. **MediaWiki API**
	* Free-tier limit: Unlimited requests for open-source projects
	* Integration effort: M (Medium)
	* Value-add: Enables seamless integration with existing MediaWiki instances, allowing users to leverage wiki-scribe's LLM-powered content generation and quality control features.
2. **Google Cloud Natural Language API**
	* Free-tier limit: 5,000 text records per month
	* Integration effort: S (Small)
	* Value-add: Enhances content analysis and quality control capabilities, providing users with more accurate and informative wiki content.
3. **Trello API**
	* Free-tier limit: 250 requests per minute
	* Integration effort: S (Small)
	* Value-add: Allows users to manage wiki content creation and editing workflows directly within Trello, streamlining project management and collaboration.
4. **Discord API**
	* Free-tier limit: 100 requests per minute
	* Integration effort: M (Medium)
	* Value-add: Enables users to receive notifications and updates on wiki content changes, fostering community engagement and encouraging collaborative content creation.
5. **Grammarly API**
	* Free-tier limit: 100 requests per day
	* Integration effort: S (Small)
	* Value-add: Provides users with advanced grammar and spell checking capabilities, ensuring high-quality and error-free wiki content.
6. **Fandom API**
	* Free-tier limit: 1,000 requests per day
	* Integration effort: M (Medium)
	* Value-add: Allows users to integrate wiki-scribe with Fandom's vast network of wikis, expanding the platform's reach and potential user base.
7. **Amazon Associates API**
	* Free-tier limit: None (revenue-share based)
	* Integration effort: L (Large)
	* Value-add: Enables users to monetize their wikis through affiliate marketing, providing a new revenue stream and incentivizing high-quality content creation.

## Prioritization
The partner integration roadmap prioritizes those with affiliate or revenue-share models, such as Amazon Associates, to create new revenue streams for wiki-scribe users. The integration effort and value-add for each partner will be continuously evaluated to ensure the best possible user experience and platform growth.
