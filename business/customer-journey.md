```markdown
# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
- **Trigger Event**: User searches for solutions to manage and create spoiler-free wiki content efficiently.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of generic wiki tools, lack of specific solutions for spoiler-free content.
- **User Emotions**: Frustrated, overwhelmed, seeking a tailored solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide targeted content marketing (blogs, SEO) highlighting the unique benefits of wiki-scribe.
- **Metric**: Number of unique visitors to the landing page.

## Consider
- **Trigger Event**: User discovers wiki-scribe through targeted ads, organic search, or word of mouth.
- **Friction Points**: Understanding the value proposition compared to existing tools, pricing concerns.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful but cautious, seeking validation.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer free trials, detailed case studies, and testimonials from similar users.
- **Metric**: Time spent on the product page, engagement with case studies.

## Try
- **Trigger Event**: User signs up for a free trial or demo of wiki-scribe.
- **Friction Points**: Onboarding complexity, initial setup time, learning curve.
- **User Emotions**: Excited but anxious about the learning curve.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide a seamless onboarding process with guided tutorials and 24/7 support.
- **Metric**: Completion rate of the onboarding process, time to first wiki creation.

## Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: User successfully creates and manages a wiki using wiki-scribe.
- **Friction Points**: Integration with existing workflows, scalability concerns.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfied but concerned about long-term usability.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer personalized onboarding sessions, regular updates, and community support.
- **Metric**: Conversion rate from trial to paid subscription, user retention rate.

## Expand
- **Trigger Event**: User experiences the benefits of wiki-scribe and considers expanding usage.
- **Friction Points**: Cost of scaling, additional features needed.
- **User Emotions**: Confident but seeking more value.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Introduce tiered pricing, additional features, and integrations with other tools.
- **Metric**: Upsell rate, number of additional wikis created, user engagement with advanced features.
```
