# REQUIREMENTS.md
## Introduction
The wiki-scribe project aims to develop a wiki-building platform that leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate high-quality, spoiler-free content and automates quality control and editing processes for solo developers and fan-wiki operators. This document outlines the functional and non-functional requirements for the wiki-scribe platform.

## Functional Requirements
1. **FR-1: User Authentication**: The platform shall provide a secure user authentication system, allowing users to create accounts, log in, and manage their profiles.
2. **FR-2: Wiki Creation**: The platform shall enable users to create new wikis, with options to customize wiki settings, such as title, description, and access permissions.
3. **FR-3: Content Generation**: The platform shall utilize LLMs to generate high-quality, spoiler-free content for wiki articles, based on user-provided topics or keywords.
4. **FR-4: Content Editing**: The platform shall provide a user-friendly content editing interface, allowing users to review, edit, and refine generated content.
5. **FR-5: Quality Control**: The platform shall automate quality control processes, including spell checking, grammar checking, and content validation, to ensure high-quality content.
6. **FR-6: Search and Navigation**: The platform shall provide a robust search function and navigation system, enabling users to easily find and access wiki articles.
7. **FR-7: Collaboration Tools**: The platform shall offer collaboration tools, such as commenting, @mentioning, and revision history, to facilitate teamwork and communication among users.
8. **FR-8: Customization Options**: The platform shall provide customization options, including themes, templates, and extensions, to enable users to personalize their wikis.
9. **FR-9: Integration with External Services**: The platform shall support integration with external services, such as social media platforms, to enable users to share their wikis and content.
10. **FR-10: Admin Dashboard**: The platform shall provide an admin dashboard, allowing administrators to manage user accounts, monitor platform activity, and perform maintenance tasks.

## Non-Functional Requirements
### Performance
* The platform shall respond to user requests within 2 seconds, on average.
* The platform shall support a minimum of 100 concurrent users, without significant performance degradation.
* The platform shall ensure that content generation and quality control processes do not exceed 10 seconds, on average.

### Security
* The platform shall implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, secure authentication, and access controls.
* The platform shall protect user data and content, in accordance with relevant data protection regulations.
* The platform shall perform regular security audits and penetration testing, to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

### Reliability
* The platform shall ensure high availability, with a minimum uptime of 99.5%.
* The platform shall provide automated backup and recovery processes, to minimize data loss in the event of a failure.
* The platform shall implement monitoring and alerting systems, to quickly detect and respond to issues.

## Constraints
* The platform shall be developed using open-source technologies and frameworks, to ensure flexibility and customizability.
* The platform shall be designed to support a minimum of 100,000 wiki articles, with the ability to scale to larger volumes.
* The platform shall comply with relevant laws and regulations, including data protection and accessibility standards.

## Assumptions
* Users shall have a basic understanding of wiki concepts and content creation principles.
* The platform shall be used primarily for creating and managing wikis, rather than for other purposes.
* The platform shall be developed and deployed within a 6-month timeframe, with ongoing maintenance and updates thereafter.
